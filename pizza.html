Classic Homemade Pizza

Ingredients

  • Pizza dough (homemade or store-bought)
  • 1/2 cup pizza sauce
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 cup sliced pepperoni
  • 1/4 cup sliced black olives
  • 1/4 cup sliced bell peppers
  • 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • Olive oil, for brushing

Instructions

Step 1: Preheat the oven to 475°F (245°C).
Step 2: Roll out the pizza dough on a floured surface to your desired thickness.
Step 3: Transfer the dough to a pizza stone or baking sheet. Brush lightly with olive oil.
Step 4: Spread the pizza sauce evenly over the dough, leaving a small border around the edges.
Step 5: Sprinkle the mozzarella cheese over the sauce. Add pepperoni, olives, and bell peppers.
Step 6: Bake in the preheated oven for 12-15 minutes or until the crust is golden and cheese is bubbly.
Step 7: Garnish with Parmesan cheese before serving.
