Classic Lasagna

Ingredients

  • 12 lasagna noodles
  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 4 cups marinara sauce
  • 15 ounces ricotta cheese
  • 3 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 tablespoon Italian seasoning
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Olive oil

Instructions

Step 1: Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
Step 2: Cook lasagna noodles in boiling water until al dente. Drain and set aside.
Step 3: In a skillet, heat olive oil over medium heat. Add the onion and ground beef, cooking until the beef is browned. Stir in marinara sauce and simmer for 5 minutes. Season with salt and pepper.
Step 4: In a bowl, mix together ricotta cheese, 2 cups mozzarella, Parmesan cheese, eggs, and Italian seasoning.
Step 5: Spread a layer of meat sauce in the bottom of a baking dish. Layer with noodles, ricotta mixture, and meat sauce. Repeat layers.
