Delicious Ramen Recipe

Ingredients

  • Noodles
  • Broth
  • Vegetables
  • Water
  • Chicken
  • Spices

Instructions

Step 1: Boil the noodles.
Step 2: Prepare the broth.
Step 3: Add vegetables and other ingredients.
Step 4: Cook the chicken in the oil for 15 minutes
Step 5: Add the chicken to the soup and let it cool down for 5 minutes
